## Featured Project Directions / 代表项目方向

### Medical SLM/RAG Evaluation

Resource-aware evaluation of local small language models and RAG systems for heart-failure discharge education QA, focusing on answer quality, medical safety, latency, memory usage, and deployment-stage energy per query.

面向心衰出院教育问答场景，评估本地小语言模型与 RAG 系统在回答质量、医疗安全、延迟、内存占用和部署阶段能耗方面的表现。

### Transfer Reinforcement Learning for Maze Path Planning

Transfer reinforcement learning project based on Q-learning and maze path planning, evaluating whether transferred knowledge can reduce repeated training cost under changed obstacle distributions.

基于 Q-learning 和迷宫路径规划的迁移强化学习项目，关注在障碍分布变化的环境中，迁移策略是否能够降低重复训练成本并提升收敛效率。

### Medical RAG Prototype

Lightweight local medical retrieval prototype using SentenceTransformer embeddings, cosine similarity search, top-k passage retrieval, and reference display for retrieved medical knowledge snippets.

轻量级本地医疗知识检索原型，使用 SentenceTransformer embedding、余弦相似度检索、top-k 参考片段召回与知识来源展示。

### Biomedical AI & Engineering Projects

Course and lab projects related to medical imaging, medical instrumentation, signal processing, neural networks, and biomedical engineering experiments.

医学图像处理、医学仪器、信号处理、神经网络和生物医学工程实验相关课程与项目。
